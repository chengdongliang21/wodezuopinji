项目1：电影翻拍《绿皮书》

★项目名称：电影翻拍《绿皮书》

Project Name: Film Remake "Green Book"

★时长：3分38秒 | 类型：剧情短片

Duration: 3 minutes 38 seconds | Type: Drama Short Film

★技术工具/Technical Tools:

  • 拍摄设备：Nikon zfc + DJI RS4 Pro

    Shooting Equipment: Nikon zfc + DJI RS4 Pro
  • 后期软件：Adobe Premiere Pro

    Post-production Software: Adobe Premiere Pro

★我的角色/My Role:

  • 导演/编剧：主导叙事结构设计，撰写详细分镜脚本，规划场景过渡和角色互动

    Director/Screenwriter: Led narrative structure design, wrote detailed storyboard, planned scene transitions and character interactions
  • 电影拍摄：采用固定机位结合镜头移动方式拍摄，精确还原电影效果，处理构图和动态调整

    Filming: Used fixed camera positions combined with moving shots to accurately reproduce film effects, handled composition and dynamic adjustments

★项目亮点/Project Highlights:

  • 忠实还原电影《绿皮书》的片段，在环境布景上模拟原片氛围，光线处理上优化自然光效，配乐上融入个人改编元素，提升情感深度

    Faithfully recreated clips from the film "Green Book", simulated the original film's atmosphere in environmental staging, optimized natural lighting, and incorporated personal adaptations in the soundtrack to enhance emotional depth.
  • 作为课程设计作品，获得92分高分，赢得专业课老师的专业认可和正面反馈

    As a course design project, it received a high score of 92, earning professional recognition and positive feedback from the specialized course instructor.

项目展示/Project Showcase

影片介绍/Film Introduction:
  • 类型：剧情片（翻拍）
    Genre: Drama (Remake)
  • 原电影片段：《绿皮书》中写信的片段
    Original Film Segment: The letter-writing scene from "Green Book"
  • 片长：3分38秒
    Duration: 3 minutes 38 seconds
  • 主题：反种族歧视
    Theme: Anti-racism
  • 主旨：通过白人与黑人在写信过程中发生的有趣对话，表现出二人的相处逐渐和谐，同时以小见大，体现了白人的种族歧视观念不断改变，黑人用自己渊博的学识和优雅的风度征服了白人的心。
    Synopsis: Through interesting conversations between a white man and a black man during letter writing, it shows their gradually harmonious relationship. It also broadly illustrates the changing racial discrimination views of the white man, as the black man's profound knowledge and elegant demeanor win over his heart.
拍摄准备/Shooting Preparation:

★拍摄地点：河南省安阳市一座公园中一处树林前的座位，与原电影中环境相似，体现写信时的静，更好的突出人物对话与动作；一处复古的室内环境，与原电影中环境相似，光线更好的体现白人妻子读信后的欣喜与感动。

Shooting Location: A seat in front of a grove in a park in Anyang City, Henan Province, similar to the original film's setting, reflecting the tranquility of letter writing to better highlight character dialogue and actions; a retro indoor environment, similar to the original film's setting, where light better reflects the white wife's joy and emotion after reading the letter.

★设备：微单相机，备用电池，三脚架，补光灯，复古灯。

Equipment: Mirrorless camera, spare batteries, tripod, fill light, retro lamp.

★人物：饰演白人的演员穿着较朴素，喜欢抽烟，可以更好的表现其文化水平一般，性格大大咧咧的人物特征，饰演黑人的演员身着黑色的衣服，表现出高于白人的审美水平和风度等。二人风格，性格不同能更好体现出反差，对比更强烈。

Characters: The actor playing the white man is dressed simply and smokes, better showcasing his average cultural level and carefree personality. The actor playing the black man is dressed in black clothes, demonstrating a higher aesthetic taste and demeanor than the white man. The differences in their styles and personalities better highlight the contrast, making it more pronounced.

★配一段优美的音乐配合影片剧情的推进。

Accompanied by beautiful music to enhance the film's progression.

后期剪辑/Post-production Editing:
  1. 画面更注重人物表情动作等特写来表现主题。
    The footage focuses more on close-ups of character expressions and actions to convey the theme.
  2. 画面切换时简洁有力，表现出双方对话的有趣场景。
    Scene transitions are concise and powerful, depicting interesting dialogue scenes between the two parties.
  3. 画面色调的统一以及画面的整洁干净。
    Uniformity of footage tones and clean, tidy visuals.
  4. 在白人妻子读信的片段以及片尾加入音乐配合剧情推进。
    Music is added to the scene where the white wife reads the letter and at the end of the film to complement the plot's progression.
  5. 在片头片片尾加入说明性字幕。
    Explanatory subtitles are added at the beginning and end of the film.
  6. 控制影片时长在合理的范围内。
    Controlling the film's duration within a reasonable range.

Project Name: "Valkyrie" - Creative Short Film Based on Classic Game Footage

★时长：1分28秒 | 类型：剪辑短片

Duration: 1 minute 28 seconds | Type: Edited Short Film

★技术工具/Technical Tools:

  • 剪辑软件：Adobe Premiere Pro——用于视频剪辑、时间线编辑和基础特效处理。

★项目亮点/Project Highlights:

Through precise beat-matching techniques, action sequences are perfectly synchronized with the background music rhythm, achieving smooth content transitions and visual impact; Additionally, dynamic transitions and color grading are used to further enhance immersion and viewing experience, highlighting the artistic expression of the work.

项目3：城市风景视频剪辑作品

★项目名称：《昆明风光：航拍下的昆明美景》

Project Name: "Kunming Scenery: Beautiful Views of Kunming from Aerial Photography"

★时长：1分27秒

Duration: 1 minute 27 seconds

★类型：剪辑短片

Type: Edited Short Film

★项目背景：该项目通过航拍技术捕捉昆明市的城市景观，旨在展现其独特的城市风景和人文风貌，适用于旅游宣传或城市形象展示。

Project Background: This project captures the urban landscape of Kunming City through aerial photography, aiming to showcase its unique urban scenery and humanistic charm, suitable for tourism promotion or urban image display.

★技术工具/Technical Tools:

  • 剪辑软件：Adobe Premiere Pro（用于主要剪辑、音视频同步及时间线管理）
    Editing Software: Adobe Premiere Pro (for main editing, audio-video synchronization, and timeline management)
  • 辅助工具：Lumetri Color进行画面调色，确保视觉效果生动
    Auxiliary Tool: Lumetri Color for color grading to ensure vivid visual effects

★项目亮点/Project Highlights:

  • 卡点剪辑精准配合城市风景转场，实现节奏感强的画面切换
    Precise beat-matching for scene transitions, achieving rhythmic screen changes
  • 融入动态镜头运动，如俯冲和环绕拍摄，增强视觉冲击力
    Incorporated dynamic camera movements, such as dive and orbit shots, to enhance visual impact
  • 音乐与场景的同步设计，提升整体观赏流畅度和情感表达
    Synchronized music and scene design to improve overall viewing fluidity and emotional expression
